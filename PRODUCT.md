# Product

## Register

product

## Users

AMBAR is used by archivists, records managers, custody operators, HR document staff, auditors, and platform administrators in Colombian enterprise SGDEA environments. Users work under operational pressure: locating files, controlling custody, validating transfers, registering evidence, enforcing TRD rules, and proving traceability.

## Product Purpose

AMBAR is an enterprise SGDEA and archival custody platform for living expedients, TRD, document typologies, physical/digital repositories, FUID, Kardex, transfers, loans, audit, and file-level permissions. Success means an authorized user can answer quickly where a unit is, who has custody, what changed, why it changed, and which evidence supports the operation.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, premium, operational, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

AMBAR must not feel like a generic CRUD admin panel, an ERP overloaded with unrelated modules, an old government form system, or a decorative dashboard with fake metrics. Avoid repeated forms, dead buttons, manual physical-location text, excessive visual effects, and simulated data in authenticated product screens.

## Design Principles

1. Operation before decoration: every screen must support a real archival action or decision.
2. Traceability is visible: custody, audit, Kardex, location, and evidence should be one click away.
3. Secure by default: permissions by archive and action govern navigation, buttons, API access, and exports.
4. Structured data beats free text: TRD, typologies, locations, roles, and physical storage are selected from controlled catalogs.
5. Premium means disciplined: calm density, clear hierarchy, fast feedback, responsive layouts, and purposeful motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for product UI. Preserve visible focus, keyboard navigation, sufficient contrast, reduced-motion support, clear empty/error states, and touch targets of at least 44px for primary controls.
